While I’m not particularly fond of the name, [musikCube](http://www.musikcube.com/) has exceeded my expectations. First of all, it’s open-source. On top of that, it has a relatively low footprint and it’s fast.

I was pleasantly surprised to see a [Last.fm](http://www.last.fm/) [plugin](http://www.musikcube.com/page/plugins/view/17).

Here are some nice features I noticed off the bat:

-   filtering while you type (like in explorer, but more robust)
-   customization of the view panels (add as many as you wish: album, artist, genre, year, etc)
-   dynamic playlists
-   tag editor with automatic capitalization features, etc

If you have some spare time, I highly recommend giving [musikCube](http://www.musikcube.com/) a whirl as an alternative music player.
