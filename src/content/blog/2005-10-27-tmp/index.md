[Matt](http://deletem3.com/) suggested a comic by the name of [Transmetropolitan](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transmetropolitan), a “postcyberpunk” (wow…) comic book series written by Warren Ellis with art by Darick Robertson. I got my copy of the first in the series yesterday and started reading almost immmediately. I didn’t get very far though because I wasn’t feeling that great. I ended up going to bed really early… dozing off with my faithful accomplice [Bird Show](http://www.kranky.net/artists/birdshow.html) projecting from the speakers.

I’m itching to get home and continue reading (laughing)! I got it from [Cosmic Cat Comics](http://cosmiccatcomics.com/) here in town, where the owner (who acts like he has a tree stump stuck up his ass) said to come back and get the rest of the series if I liked it.

My thoughts so far on Transmetropolitan are vague, but it is clear that Spider is one **angry** journalist! Phew…
