You may or may not remember Soulseek. It was a file sharing application that was
originally built for Windows. What I loved about the original Soulseek was _how_
you found music. It wasn't fed to you via an algorithm. You found stuff by
peering into real people's collections. You could usually find related stuff
just based on a person's tastes. I found so much good music that way and I miss
that kind of exploration.

While I still love the original Soulseek, I wanted to use Claude to make my own
version that runs solely in a browser that met these needs and just see what was
possible. Imagine if Slack/Discord allowed real-time audio streaming.

Check it out here: https://livingroom-fm.fly.dev/ and come browse human-curated music!
