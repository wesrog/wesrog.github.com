Frittata recipe, if you ever forget:

-   Preheat oven to around 375
-   Sautee veggies of your choice in a tbsp or 2 of butter (mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, soy crumbles or a meat of your choice)
-   6 eggs, whisked, beaten and seasoned with salt, garlic pepper and veggie seasoning
-   1 TON OF CHEESE
-   ½ cup of evaporated milk
-   Mix it all up in a pyrex dish thing and cook for 25 mins.
-   After done, take it out and let it cool for a couple mins.

Damn good! You’ll have leftovers for a couple mornings.
