I love [Volumouse](http://www.nirsoft.net/utils/volumouse.html). I use it at work a ton because I don’t have a volume control on my mouse.

I find it burdensome to activate a media player window in order to change the volume. With [Volumouse](http://www.nirsoft.net/utils/volumouse.html) you can specify a hot key to hold down when rolling the mouse wheel to change the system volume. It comes in handy when you need to immediately mute or lower the volume.
