Hey. Look at the slick new chart to the left. Thanks [Last.fm](http://www.last.fm/onyoursite/banners.php)!

I’m not happy with the audioscrobbler sidebar plugin that comes with Typo. This is a lot nicer, but it isn’t very interactive. It’s just a static image. I’m not complaining!

If you haven’t already, I highly recommend getting an account at [Last.fm](http://www.last.fm/signup.php). It gives you a detailed analysis of what you listen to. Charts, graphs, recommendations, the whole nine…all transparently! Be wary though, some recommendations are waaaay off. That’s to be expected, until quantum computing and holographic memory, of course.

*EDIT:* The new recommendations system, codenamed [Pony](http://www.last.fm/recommended/) is proving to be quite resourceful and more accurate. They are really starting to add in lots of cool new features.
