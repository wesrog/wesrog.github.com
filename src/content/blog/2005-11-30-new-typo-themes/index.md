I’ve been keeping a close eye on the typo theme contest, and trying to convice [Matt](http://www.deletem3.com/) to design one, but alas… the contest has ended and no theme from him. While there are some great ones out there (Matt, you could’ve made a great one!), there are minor discrepancies that prevent me from using a particular theme. Except one. I am partial to [Scribbish](http://typogarden.com/articles/2005/11/09/scribbish-theme-and-theme-template). It’s consistent. Props to the [author](http://quotedprintable.com/articles/2005/11/07/scribbish-0-1).

I’m now anxiously awaiting the results to see who wins the [Typo Theme Contest](http://typogarden.com/)
